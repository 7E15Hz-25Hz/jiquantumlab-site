Ji Quantum Lab

Seeing quantum materials in the microwave world.

We combine microwave quantum transport, microwave imaging, and programmable quantum phases to understand and control correlated and topological states in 2D materials.

Program Quantum Phases

Use gates, strain, and artificial lattices to design and control correlated and topological states in 2D materials beyond moiré.

Microwave Quantum Transport

Explore quantum transport at GHz frequencies to probe dynamics and collective modes that DC measurements can’t see.

Microwave Quantum Imaging

Build scanning probes that image local phases, domains, and edges using microwave and millimeter-wave signals.
